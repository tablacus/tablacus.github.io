Check box

Function

Use a check box beside each item for selection

Note

  1. To use this add-on, you must select “double click to open (single click to select)” in “Folder Options” of Windows control panel. If you select “single click to open (hover to select)”, selection may lose at mouse movement.
  2. “Use check box to select” option in “Folder Options” of Windows control panel has no effect in TE.
