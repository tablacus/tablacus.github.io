Tab plus

Advanced tab.

Function

Functions of "Tabs" add-on, plus:

  1. Icon on tab
  2. “Close” button on tab
  3. “New tab” button in tab area
  4. Background color to highlight the active pane

Note

  • The tabs appear in Aero style.
  • Customizations through "Tools > Options > Tabs" do not apply to this add-on. Use “Tab design” add-on if needed.
  • When this add-on is enabled, "Tabs" add-on can be disabled.
